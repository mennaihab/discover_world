The Most Common Animals

Giraffe

Giraffe is a tall African mammal belonging to the genus Giraffa. Specifically, It is an even-toed ungulate. It is the tallest living terrestrial animal and the largest ruminant on Earth.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest existing land animals. Three living species are currently recognised:the African bush elephant, the African forest elephant,and the Asian elephant.

Horse

horse (Equus ferus caballus) is a domesticated one-toed hoofed mammal. It belongs to the taxonomic family Equidae and is one of two extant subspecies of Equus ferus.
